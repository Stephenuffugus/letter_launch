How to play

Aim & drop: drag anywhere (down / down-left / down-right), then release. The glowing column shows exactly where the tile will land — the dotted line is the real path, so placement is all skill. Longer drag = more power.

Spell two ways: trace across touching tiles (incl. diagonals), or tap tiles one at a time and press ✓ to submit. Tap a picked tile to unpick it; ✕ clears. Words are 3+ letters; valid words clear and the stack drops.

Streak: clear another word before the timer runs out to build a multiplier — x2, x3, and up.

Charge: bounce a tile off a gold ★ bumper and it lands charged — worth double its letter value in any word.

Levels (the main mode): you’re dealt exactly the tiles to build the words on the checklist — clear the list to win, then climb. Stuck? Tap ↻ to retry.

Power-ups (cost coins): 🔀 shuffle the board’s letters, 🔁 swap two tiles, 💣 bomb a tile. Buttons sit at the bottom while you play.

Word Hunt: launch letters to fill the board, then spell the word it can make. Stuck? Take a letter — but each extra one drops a black block. Clear a word next to a block to break it.

Switch modes: tap the ☰ pill (top bar) any time for the home menu — Levels, Word Hunt, Daily Climb, Daily, or Free.
