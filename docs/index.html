How to play

Drop: drag in the direction you want to fire (down / down-left / down-right) anywhere on screen, then release. The tile falls through the bumpers into a column. Longer drag = more power.

Spell: drag across touching tiles (incl. diagonals) to trace a word (3+ letters). Valid words clear and the stack drops.

Streak: clear another word before the timer runs out to build a multiplier — x2, x3, and up.
